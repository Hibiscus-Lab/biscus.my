About Us

Hibiscus Lab has an audacious but simple vision: to discover, develop and deliver a new cybersecurity revolution. Hibiscus Lab will use cybersecurity as a catalyst as an economic growth. Estimation around 20,000 professionals in the cybersecurity needed by 2025 in Malaysia. Hibiscus Lab will fill the gap by producing multiple non-techs to IT security experts within short time. The evolution of cybersecurity has been continuing to expand and grow, and cybercriminals will be right behind these new trends. The biggest challenge that most companies are facing that are into space; whether they're supporting government or whether they are securing their own system, is still not finding the right talent. The law of the certification was developed 10 or 15 years ago which may be unrelated to today cybersecurity technology. Our hope to use this critical moment in history to strengthen collaboration and cooperation nationally, regionally, and internationally – public and private sector together. 

Vision

Discover

discover a world where cybersecurity is a fundamental aspect of daily life, where individuals are empowered to protect themselves and their data, and where organizations prioritize cybersecurity as a core business function.

Develop

develop a community where cybersecurity knowledge is freely shared, and individuals from diverse backgrounds and skill levels can come together to learn, collaborate, and innovate to improve cybersecurity for all.

Deliver

deliver a culture of cybersecurity consciousness, where individuals and organizations are motivated and supported to make cybersecurity a top priority in their personal and professional lives.

Mission

Educate

Developing educational resources and workshops to teach people about the importance of cybersecurity and how to protect themselves online

Hosting

Hosting community events to bring together individuals, businesses, and organizations to discuss cybersecurity challenges and collaborate on solutions

Partnership

Developing partnerships with cybersecurity experts, organizations, and government agencies to support and promote cybersecurity initiatives at a local and national level.
